Welcome

Elegant Choices is an annual conference for teenagers and young adults; an initiative of Taiwo Oluburode (CEO, Empowered You Network).

Elegant Choices is a fun, educative and informative event organised and facilitated by three non-profit organisations (Empowered You Network, Some1Nigeria and OJOFEL) that are adherently devoted to building and equipping the next generation of purpose-driven individuals.

We do hope you have a hitch-free time checking our site. Kindly contact us for further information.

It aims at developing the minds of the participants towards making the right choices in life. We have been committed to this cause and have impacted the lives of over 2000 teenagers and young adults positively.

Prof. L.O. Kehinde, Kayode Makinde, Prof. K. Alao, Prof.(Mrs.) K. Taiwo, Prof.(Mrs.) B. Adedeji, Baiye Adebowale, Rotimi Kehinde, Folake Orafidiya, Ighodaro Omoghui, Taiwo Oluborode among others have been facilitators during previous events.

This year, at Elegant Choices 2018 we have a goal of reaching out to 500 teenagers and young adults who will after the conference:

  • Leave with clear minds and goals; already thought out plans on actualising the goals.
  • Have created important connections and relationships which will be long-lasting and have undeniable positive effects in their lives.
  • Begin to see beyond just themselves and recognise their part to play in the building of the society.
  • Be able to understand the most important factors that guide the making of life decisions; and
  • Be able to stand by their decisions and take responsibility for the outcomes of their decision.

Elegant Choices 2018 is scheduled to hold on 29th May 2018(Democracy Day) at Obafemi Awolowo University Conference Centre.
